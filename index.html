Ocean Sustainability and Electrical Engineering

Welcome! The goal of Ocean Sustainability and Electrical Engineering is to act as an educational resources for electrical engineering students to learn about how their work can be related to ocean sustainability. This project aims to address the lack of sustainability and climate-related education in electrical engineering program curriculums across Canada, despite sustainability being a major impact area in electrical engineering.
